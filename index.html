Serving custom font to client: Marker Felt

Marker Felt

In this page, I'll share my experience in serving a unique font, Marker Felt, to web browsers.

Step-by-Step Guide

  • Font Selection

    Start by selecting an exceptional font for your website. Marker Felt was my choice.

  • Font File Acquisition

    Obtain the necessary font files in .woff and .woff2 formats.

  • Uploading to the Server

    Upload these font files to your web server, which is like giving your website access to them.

  • Styling with Custom Font

    Now, with Curlz MT at your disposal, style your website text. It adds a unique touch!

  • Sharing Your Journey

    Create a webpage like this one to share your font-serving adventure and let others see Curlz MT in action.
